Travel App

Interaction Design – Visual Design – Illustrations

Lëtzebuerg is a Travel App that focuses on Luxembourg’s most beautiful and memorable landmarks.

For this project I wanted to create a Travel App unique to my country of origin and I achieved this by making hand drawn illustrations using watercolour.

This project took me 6 weeks to complete, with some touch-ups afterwards.

I focused on the illustrations in this project, so the main challenge was to make watercolour illustrations pop in this app.

I achieved this by using stone paper for the illustrations which is less porous as opposed to regular water colour paper, this allowed me to take a clear scan of the illustrations true to their colour.

Another constraint is space: eliminating unnecessary content and sticking to the important content allowed me to create a clean layout.

The final App is called Lëtzebuerg and it is very unique because of those watercolour illustrations.
